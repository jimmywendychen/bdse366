Avocado tuna melt bites 
Ingredients:
10 oz. Canned Tuna, drained
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 medium Avocado, cubed
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/3 cup Almond Flour
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp. Onion Powder
Salt and Pepper to Taste
1/2 cup Coconut Oil, for frying (~1/4 cup absorbed) 
Method:
STEP 1
Drain a can of tuna and add it to a large container where you’ll be able to mix everything together.
STEP 2
Add mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, and spices to the tuna and mix together well.
STEP 3
Slice an avocado in half, remove the pit, and cube the inside.
STEP 4
Add avocado into the tuna mixture and fold together, trying to not mash the avocado into the mixture.
STEP 5
Form the tuna mixture into balls and roll into almond flour, covering completely. Set aside.
STEP 6
Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat. Once hot, add tuna balls and fry until crisp on all sides.
STEP 7
Remove from the pan and serve.
Nutrients:
1. 135 Calories
2. 11.8 Fats
3. 0.8g Net Carbs
4. 6.2g Protein
